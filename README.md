# 🏦 Bank Analytics — Loan Portfolio, Transaction & Risk Analysis

A comprehensive multi-dimensional analysis of a banking dataset covering loan portfolios, credit-debit transaction flows, branch performance, risk exposure, and fraud indicators.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes banking operations across **66,000+ loan accounts** and millions of transactions to uncover risk patterns, customer behavior, and operational inefficiencies. The analysis spans SQL querying, Excel modeling, Power BI dashboards, and Tableau visualizations.

---

## 🔑 Key Insights

- **Balanced Transaction Flows** — Credit (₹127.60M) and debit (₹127.29M) show near-perfect equilibrium (ratio: 1.00), indicating financial stability
- **Loan Portfolio** — Total loan value of $751M across 66K loans; collections at $809M with a low default rate of ~1.56%
- **Age Group 26–35 dominates** — 50%+ of borrowers; 36-month maturity loans preferred by 95.7% of customers
- **Top Performing Cities** — Mathura (₹31M), Sangrur (₹25M), Agra (₹21M)
- **Geographic Concentration** — Heavy exposure in Uttar Pradesh → diversification risk identified
- **Verification vs Default** — Unverified accounts (16,548) drive the majority of defaults; KYC gaps are a critical risk driver
- **High-Risk Transactions** — ~20% of flagged activity identified; seasonal dips may mask fraud patterns

## 📋 Strategic Recommendations

1. **Risk Management** — Deploy behavior-based early warning systems; recalibrate default models by customer segment
2. **Growth Optimization** — Target 26–35 demographic and high-performing cities; adjust product pricing by branch profitability
3. **Collections Strategy** — Use digital reminders, hardship programs, and weekly cure-rate monitoring
4. **Fraud/AML Prevention** — Strengthen detection rules during seasonal dips; monitor suspicious transaction spikes
5. **Enhanced KYC** — Verification status is the strongest predictor of default → stricter onboarding needed
